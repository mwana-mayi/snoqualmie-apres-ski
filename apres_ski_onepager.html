Building the Next Generation of Mountain Community

The Story

Two years ago, we discovered skiing and snowboarding for the first time. Coming from a place like the Democratic Republic of the Congo — where snow sports simply don't exist — stepping onto the mountain was transformative. It created joy, friendships, and a sense of belonging.

We realized something powerful: many people in our communities have never even considered winter sports possible for them. This event is about changing that.

Our vision is to create a welcoming cultural après‑ski experience that introduces new communities to Snoqualmie Pass — and grows into an annual tradition bringing new audiences to the mountain every year.
