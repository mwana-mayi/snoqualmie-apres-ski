# Snoqualmie Après-Ski

A one-page proposal for a **Community Ski Day & Cultural Après-Ski** event at The Summit at Snoqualmie.

## About

This proposal introduces a vision for bringing new, diverse communities to winter sports through a welcoming ski day paired with a cultural après-ski celebration featuring live DJ sets, African diaspora music, and social gathering.

### Key highlights

- **50+ projected attendees** traveling from 7+ states
- **80% beginners or first-time skiers** — a strong new-customer conversion opportunity
- Multicultural community representing Congolese, Nigerian, Ghanaian, Brazilian, Colombian, and many other backgrounds
- Morning ski/snowboard sessions followed by an afternoon outdoor DJ and cultural dance experience
